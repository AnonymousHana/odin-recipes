Hana's vegetable Salad

Here is my own vegetable salad I like to share with you guys.

List of ingredients

    • 2 cups mixed greens (such as spinach, arugula, and lettuce)
    • 1 medium cucumber, sliced
    • 1 medium carrot, shredded
    • 1 medium tomato, chopped
    • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
    • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Making the Salad

    In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, carrot, tomato, and red onion.
    In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper.
    Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
    Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad.
You can also customize this salad by adding other vegetables or toppings that you enjoy, such as bell peppers, avocado, or nuts.

Voila! And here you just made Hana's vegetable salad. Enjoy!
